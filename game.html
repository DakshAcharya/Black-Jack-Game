Blackjack Game

In Blackjack, your goal is to beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without going over. You start with two cards and can choose to "Hit" to get another card or "Stand" to keep your current hand. The dealer also gets two cards, with one face down. If you go over 21, you lose. If the dealer goes over 21, you win. Otherwise, the higher hand wins.
