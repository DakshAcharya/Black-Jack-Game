Blackjack Rules

  • Objective: Beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without going over
  • Card Values: Number cards (2-10) = face value, Face cards (J,Q,K) = 10, Aces = 1 or 11
  • Your Turn Options: "Hit" to receive another card, "Stand" to keep your current hand
  • Dealer's Rules: Dealer must hit until reaching 17 or higher
  • Busting: If your hand exceeds 21, you automatically lose your bet
  • Winning Conditions: Beat the dealer's hand without busting, or win if dealer busts
  • Ties: Equal hand values result in a "push" - your bet is returned
